## What we build

A-Ventures Global is an AI studio focused on **commerce** — the place where agentic AI meets real revenue. We design agentic systems, ship development tools, and publish open metrics and standards for measuring AI where it actually touches the P&L. Our work spans enterprise Amazon and marketplace commerce, inference unit economics, and the tooling that makes AI-driven commerce measurable and accountable.

We open-source the **interfaces, specs, and conventions** of this work — the measuring sticks the category can adopt — while building the systems that run on top of them.

*More open tools and standards are on the way — agentic-commerce infrastructure, marketplace tooling, and the metrics that make AI-driven commerce legible.*

## What we care about

- **Agentic commerce** — the emerging stack where AI agents make commerce decisions, and what it takes to do that profitably.
- **Inference unit economics** — treating AI spend as a line item that has to earn its keep, not a magic cost center.
- **Open metrics & standards** — publishing the vocabulary the category measures itself by.
- **Enterprise marketplace commerce** — deep roots in Amazon 1P/Vendor and the operational discipline serious brands require.
